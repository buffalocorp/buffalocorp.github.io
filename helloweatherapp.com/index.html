Dealing with the weather is hard enough. Seeing the forecast should be easy.

There are a million weather apps out there, and they're overloaded with ugly ads, confusing interfaces, and stupid gimmicks. We think that stinks, so we made the antidote: a straightforward app that’s a joy to use.

Five reasons why you’ll love planning your day with Hello Weather.

1

All the info you need is right in your face.

Our gorgeous, information-rich design shows you everything that matters in one simple screen. You’ll see the current conditions and future forecast in an instant, with more details just one tap away.

2

You won’t waste time looking at useless stuff.

Hello Weather intelligently adapts to changing conditions. When it's windy, foggy, or stormy, you’ll see all the relevant details right upfront. When conditions improve, it’s all tucked neatly out of the way again.

3

You’ll get predictions you can trust.

Hello Weather is more than a pretty face. It’s powered by the world’s best data sources: Dark Sky, Weather Underground, AccuWeather, and The Weather Channel. Pick the provider that's best in your area, or switch back and forth to compare. (Upgrade required.)

4

You don’t need to be a weather expert.

What does barometric pressure mean? Is the dewpoint good or bad? We translated those esoteric numbers into human words, so you’ll know how it really feels outside.

5

It’ll make you smile.

We filled the app with tons of thoughtful little touches to brighten your day. You’ll love the custom Home Screen icons, beautiful themes, automatic night mode, and sweet secret extras.

Exclusive Features

Radar

A sweet and simple radar view so you can see exactly what precipitation is coming your way. Available in the US, Canada, and Australia.

Forecast Data Sources

Switch from Dark Sky’s forecasts to Weather Underground, AccuWeather, AerisWeather, or The Weather Channel.

Real-Time Precipitation Estimates

When it’s raining or snowing, you’ll see an extra chart showing how strong the precipitation will be for the next hour. Available in the US, UK, and Ireland.

Night Mode Controls

Want Hello Weather to stay bright or dark all the time? Flip a switch and keep it exactly how you like it.

Custom App Icons (iOS only)

If you’re finicky about your home screen, you’ll love this. Choose your favorite and make your icon shine.

Forecast Notifications (Android only)

Who wants to open an app to check the weather? Turn on notifications and get forecast info automatically delivered right to you.
